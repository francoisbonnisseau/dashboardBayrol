# Product

## Register

product

## Users
The primary users are Bayrol internal teams who monitor and improve assistant behavior across the FR, DE, and ES bots. They work in an operational context, switch between bots often, and need fast, reliable tooling to iterate prompts and validate model behavior before publishing changes.

## Product Purpose
This dashboard centralizes day-to-day quality and content operations for Bayrol conversational assistants. Success means teams can test, compare, and validate model output quickly, while preserving a controlled promotion path from experiments to production prompts.

## Brand Personality
- Expert
- Practical
- Trustworthy

The interface should feel precise and calm, with minimal friction during repetitive testing and validation tasks.

## Anti-references
- Over-decorated marketing-style dashboards with heavy gradients and oversized hero blocks.
- Chat UIs that mimic consumer messaging apps instead of operational tooling.
- Novel or playful interactions that hide critical controls or status.

## Design Principles
1. Keep operational focus, make key actions obvious and one-click away.
2. Preserve continuity across views, reuse patterns and vocabulary already established in the app.
3. Separate experimentation from publication, require explicit validation for state-changing actions.
4. Optimize for comparison, show equivalent information side by side without cognitive overload.
5. Fail transparently, errors should be visible, actionable, and local to the failing action.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA for contrast and focus visibility.
- Preserve keyboard accessibility for all primary controls.
- Avoid motion-heavy transitions and rely on clear textual status for async operations.
